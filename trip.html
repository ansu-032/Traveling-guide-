Popular Destinations

jaipur

Jaipur, Rajasthan: Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is a vibrant and culturally rich city in the state of Rajasthan. It is famous for its magnificent palaces, such as the Hawa Mahal and the City Palace, as well as the stunning Amber Fort. Jaipur also offers an opportunity to explore local markets, taste delicious Rajasthani cuisine, and witness traditional folk performances.

kerala

Kerala: Located on the southwestern coast of India, Kerala is a tropical paradise known for its serene backwaters, palm-fringed beaches, and lush green landscapes. Visitors can enjoy a houseboat cruise through the backwaters of Alleppey, relax on the pristine beaches of Kovalam, explore the tea plantations in Munnar, and experience the rich cultural heritage of cities like Kochi and Thiruvananthapuram.

Varanasi

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh: Varanasi, situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River, is one of the oldest living cities in the world and holds immense spiritual significance for Hindus. It is renowned for its ghats, where pilgrims gather to perform religious rituals and witness captivating evening ceremonies. Varanasi also offers a glimpse into the rich cultural traditions of India, with its narrow lanes, bustling markets, and vibrant street life.
